# Financial-Analysis
This report presents a comprehensive financial analysis and comparative study of two leading Indian companies from contrasting sectors — Tata Motors Limited (NSE: TMCV) from the automobile industry and ICICI Prudential Life Insurance Company Limited (NSE: ICICIPRULI) from the insurance sector.
The study covers two timeframes: a detailed week-on-week stock performance analysis for the period April 14–20, 2026, and a three-year historical financial review spanning FY 2021-22 to FY 2023-24. Real-time market data, company filings, and analyst reports have been used to ensure accuracy and relevance.
The report is structured across eight analytical sections — weekly price movement, historical financials, ratio analysis, comparative scoring, SWOT analysis, future outlook, and a visual charts dashboard. Key metrics examined include revenue growth, net profit trends, profitability and liquidity ratios, debt levels, valuation multiples, and sector-specific indicators such as VNB margin and solvency ratio for ICICI Prudential, and EV market share and debt-to-equity deleveraging for Tata Motors.
The objective of this project is to evaluate the financial health, growth trajectory, risk profile, and investment potential of both companies, and to arrive at a data-driven verdict on their suitability for different investor profiles. Tata Motors emerges as a high-growth, high-risk opportunity driven by its EV leadership and JLR recovery, while ICICI Prudential stands out as a stable, consistent compounder with strong capital adequacy.
Data Sources: NSE India, BSE India, SEBI filings, TickerTape, Screener.in, company investor presentations.
